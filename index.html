HPO Explorer

A free web app for the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) — browse ~19,800 phenotype terms and extract HPO terms from clinical notes right in your browser.

What it does

  • Clinical note → HPO terms. In-browser NLP reads free text and maps it to HPO concepts, handling negation ("no seizures"), family history, and uncertainty. Nothing is uploaded.
  • Interactive ontology browser. Search, an expandable hierarchy tree, and a graph view over the full ontology.
  • Analysis. Phenotypic similarity, candidate-disease matching (Orphanet), and gene prioritization (NCBI), weighted by how specific each finding is.
  • Interoperable export. CSV, JSON, and GA4GH Phenopacket for Exomiser / LIRICAL.
  • For everyone. A plain-language medical-term explainer and a "which conditions feature these traits" explorer for the general public.

Not the HPOExplorer R package

This is a standalone web application at hpoexplorer.com — not the neurogenomics/HPOExplorer R/Bioconductor package, which shares a similar name.
